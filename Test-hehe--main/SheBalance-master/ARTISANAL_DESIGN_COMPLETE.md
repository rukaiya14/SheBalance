## ‚úÖ What Was Implemented

### 1. Color Palette - Earth Tones
Replaced the purple/blue tech palette with warm, organic colors:

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Cream | #F5F5DC | Main background |
| Beige | #E8DCC4 | Card backgrounds, accents |
| Deep Brown | #4B3621 | Primary text, buttons |
| Chocolate | #6B4423 | Secondary text, hover states |
| Terracotta | #C97D60 | Ratings, highlights |
| Wheat | #D4A574 | Gradients, borders |

### 2. Typography - Elegant & Professional
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) - Creates premium, artisanal feel
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) - Clean, modern readability
- **Sizes**: 3.5rem hero, 2.5rem sections, 1.5rem cards
- **Weight**: Bold (700) for headings, Medium (500-600) for body

### 3. Visual Elements

#### Shapes
- **Border Radius**: 20-25px for cards (organic, soft)
- **Buttons**: 50px (pill-shaped, friendly)
- **Inputs**: 15px (subtle rounding)

#### Shadows
- Subtle brown-toned shadows: `rgba(75, 54, 33, 0.08-0.3)`
- Soft elevation, not harsh black shadows
- Increased shadow on hover for depth

#### Spacing
- 80px vertical padding for sections (generous breathing room)
- 35px gaps between cards (not cramped)
- 25-30px padding inside cards (comfortable)

### 4. Components Redesigned

#### Hero Section
- Gradient background: Beige to Wheat
- Large decorative wheat emoji (ghosted)
- Pill-shaped location input
- Deep brown buttons with hover lift

#### Food Cards
- Cream background with white info section
- Gradient emoji placeholders (Wheat to Terracotta)
- Playfair Display for food names and prices
- Terracotta star ratings
- Pill-shaped order buttons

#### Category Cards
- White background on cream
- 3.5rem emoji icons
- Terracotta border on hover
- 8px lift animation

#### Modals
- 25px border radius (soft corners)
- Brown-tinted backdrop with blur
- Playfair Display headings
- Beige input backgrounds
- Rotating close button

#### Notifications
- Pill-shaped (50px radius)
- Deep brown/Terracotta backgrounds
- Slide-in animation from right

### 5. Animations & Interactions
- **Smooth transitions**: 0.3-0.4s cubic-bezier
- **Hover lifts**: -8px for cards, -2px for buttons
- **Entrance animations**: slideUp, fadeIn, slideIn
- **Micro-interactions**: Rotating close button, shadow growth

## üéØ Key Design Decisions

### Why Playfair Display?
- Elegant serif that conveys premium quality
- Commonly used in artisanal food branding
- Excellent readability at large sizes
- Creates emotional connection with "handmade"

### Why Earth Tones?
- Evokes natural, organic, homemade feelings
- Warm and inviting, not cold tech
- Associated with quality ingredients
- Timeless, not trendy

### Why Pill-Shaped Buttons?
- Friendly and approachable
- Modern but not overly tech
- Soft, organic feel
- Easy to tap on mobile

### Why Generous Spacing?
- Creates premium, high-end feel
- Reduces cognitive load
- Focuses attention on content
- Breathing room = quality
