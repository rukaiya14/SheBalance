## üé® Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Brand Voice
- **Warm**: Like a friend sharing a recipe
- **Authentic**: Honest about ingredients and process
- **Empowering**: Celebrating women's achievements
- **Premium**: Quality without pretension
- **Community-Focused**: We're in this together

### Writing Style
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and clear
- Include sensory details (taste, smell, texture)
- Tell stories, not just features
- Emphasize human connection

### Words to Use
- Artisan, handmade, crafted, authentic
- Fresh, seasonal, local, organic
- Traditional, time-honored, heritage
- Love, care, passion, dedication
- Community, support, empower

### Words to Avoid
- Industrial, mass-produced, factory
- Artificial, processed, instant
- Generic, ordinary, basic
- Corporate, commercial, business
- Cheap, discount, bargain
